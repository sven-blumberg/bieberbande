Beaver Gang

The Clever Card Swap!

Quick Rules

  • Each player has 4 face-down cards – you may peek at the 2 outer ones at the start
  • Goal: Collect the lowest card values possible
  • Each turn: Draw a card from the pile, swap or discard it
  • Special cards: Peek, Swap, Draw Two
  • Crazy cards: Chaos Beaver, Spy, Lightning, Tornado, Freeze, Mirror, Thief, Lucky
  • Knock = trigger the final round
